Behind the Computer Screens

The Foodie Craftsman was conceived, created, and is being maintained by Kevin Christopher Co, a food enthusiast and currently a computer student in Tuitt Philippines Incorporated. The mission of The Foodie Craftsman is to act as a learning hub to impart the different aspects of making food taste good. By teaching these key aspects to people who are serious with cooking, they will be encouraged to be open to feedback after letting others sample the fruits of their labor.



The Foodie Craftsman blog site focuses on the following core values below in making good quality food:

Ingredient

Every single component affects the dish in terms of its texture, flavor, and appearance.

Technique

One should refine his approach to a certain step in creating his dish such as knifework.


Timing

It is a must to implement a cooking method on a dish in groups such as baking the first batch of cookies longer than the second.


The vision of The Foodie Craftsman is to have people become better cooks in their own right with the knowledge that making exceptional food requires practice. Only through repetition can we be a master in our own craft.
