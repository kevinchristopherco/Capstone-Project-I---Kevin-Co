Dishes

Baked Cookies with Marshmallows (Attempt #1)

Ingredients

  • ½ cup / 122 g butter, salted (Magnolia Butter)
  • ½ cup muscovado sugar (Nature’s Muscovado Speciality Sugar)
  • ½ cup coco sugar (Coco Natural Organic Coco Sugar)
  • 1 piece. whole egg at room temperature
  • 1 tsp. vanilla extract (McCormick Vanilla Flavor)
  • 1 ½ cup all purpose flour (Magnolia Flour)
  • 1 ½ cup oatmeal (Quaker Instant Oatmeal Original)
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon powder
  • 6 tablespoon marshmallows (Mello Fluffy Assorted Softmallow)
  • 1 teaspoon. baking soda (Arm & Hammer Pure Baking Soda)
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder (Queen Double Acting Baking Powder)
  • ½ teaspoon iodized fine salt (McCormick Iodized Salt)

Procedure

  1. I mixed the butter and sugar until they became light and fluffy.

  2. The eggs were added then the vanilla extract to the butter – sugar mixture until well-blended.

  3. On a separate bowl, the dry ingredients, flour, cinnamon powder, baking soda, baking powder were sifted then combined together with Oats and salt.

  4. The marshmallows were then added to the dry ingredients mixture.

  5. The wet and dry ingredients were combined together until the new mixture became doughy in large bowl.

  6. The newly formed dough was molded to small circular shaped pieces using an ice cream scooper.

  7. Two sheets of non-stick wax paper were placed on two metal baking trays. Salted butter was brushed on the metal trays so that the cookie batter would not stick.

  8. The cookie dough was baked at 175 degrees Celsius for approximately 16:00 minutes

Feedback

  • Texture – a little chewy on some parts and some parts a dry
  • Taste and Flavor – just the right amount of sweetness and balanced flavor however cinnamon is quite concentrated on some parts
  • Appearance – there cracks found on some areas of the cookies

    • Recommendations

    • Corn Light Syrup can be used to make the cookies more chewy
    • Cocoa powder can be used instead of cinnamon since cinnamon can be clumped in certain areas
    • Cacao tablets can be melted in hot cream to provide a glazed look for the cookies
    • Softmallows can be used as they come in very small sizes making them easier to cut.
