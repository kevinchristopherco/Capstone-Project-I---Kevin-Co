Ingredients

Instant Oatmeal

400g P66.00 - P72.00

Instant Oatmeal is made of processed rolled oats and contains gluten. It can give a cereal like texture when making desserts like cookies. Oatmeal needs to be cooked well otherwise it will be painful to eat.


Muscavado Sugar

500g P66.25 - P68.00

A kind of sugar that is used to provide honey and caramel taste to the mixture.


Processed Cheese Spread

165g P72.80 - P85.00

The kind of cheese is a process filled cheese spread and has a high melting rate when heated, making it a good choice when making sauces.


Salted Butter

100g P59.00 - P70.00

This type of butter contains salt and makes the mixture creamy and rich. When spread across a pan or tray, there is less chance for the dish to stick or be burnt from the bottom.


Unsalted Butter

225g P120.00 - P149.00

This type of butter is used when salt is to be controlled. It makes the mixture creamy and rich like its salt counterpart. When spread across a pan or tray, there is less chance for the dish to stick or be burnt from the bottom.


Vanilla Extract

20ml P37.50 - P50.00

An extract that provides artificial vanilla flavour and is mainly used in cakes, desserts, icings, beverages and candies.


Vegetable Oil

100ml P9.00 - P10.00

A cholesterol-free kind of cooking oil that contains Vitamin A. This is a healthy alternative when it comes to cooking vegan dishes.


White Potato

1.070kg P114.49

This kind of potato is among the highest energy producing veggies due to containing high amounts of fibre. The white potato can be used to make dishes like salads, chips, fries, meat pies.
