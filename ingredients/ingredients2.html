Ingredients

Confectioner Sugar

450g P49.00 - P51.00

Composed mainly of sugar and cornstarch, this ingredient is used for frosting, icings, toppings, and glazing.


Eggs

462g P50.00 - P55.00/6 pieces/

Composed of an egg white and a yolk, this ingredient acts like a binder in incorporating other ingredients together.


Evaporated Milk

250ml P27.50 - P29.50

This dairy product is mainly used to make omelettes, champorado, and can also serve as the base of a cheese sauce.


Fine Salt

140g P28.00 - P30.00

This kind of salt contains iodine that is a known necessary nutrient. The fine texture of the salt lessens the chance of any clumps in the mixture.


Fluffy Assorted Softmallows

72g P27.00 - P30.00

Softmallows are small mini-sized marshmallows that serve to provide food coloring. They can be added to batter mixtures but in moderation as they can explode during baking time.


Full Cream Milk

200ml P23.00 - P26.00

This milk can assist in creating a creamy and moist mixture. It can also be used in making icing or frosting.


Granulated Sugar

1kg P65.00 - P70.00

This ingredient is used add sweetness to the dish and is fine in texture to avoid crystalized sugar clumps.


Instant Coffee Powder

25g P16.00 - P20.00

This is made from grinded coffee beans so that it would be possible to dissolve and blend into solvents like water. Instant Coffee or Soluble Coffee can add latte flavour to fillings and icings when making desserts.
