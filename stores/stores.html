Stores

Boxination


Description: This baking store sells products like confectionary sugar, candies, cookies, almonds. Its flagship products are the boxes. Bread flour and wax paper are also readily available in this store.

Location: 13 West Avenue, Quezon City

Chocolate Lovers


Description: This baking store appears as a huge chocolate castle like place. It sells products like confectioners sugar , cupcake holder sheets and other baking goods.

Location: 45 P. Tuazon Blvd, Quezon City

Puregold Supermarket (Quezon Avenue Branch)


Description: The Puregold Supermarket along QA houses a huge variety of products such as baking needs, cereal, bread, dairy, meat, fruit, vegetables. This is one of the known Puregold branches that I feel is a good source of Cavendish bananas since they are always found protected in plastic.

Location: Quezon Avenue, Quezon City
